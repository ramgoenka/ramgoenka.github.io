About me

Hi! My name is Ram Goenka. I am a first year masters student at the University of Pittsburgh in the Department of Statistics.

Prior to joining Pitt, I was an undergraduate student in Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Illinois (UIUC). I worked as a research assistant at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications on computational problems relating to the mathematical and statistical modeling of vector-borne diseases, specifically the West Nile Virus (WNV), mentored by Professor Rebecca Lee Smith.

I am primarily interested in statistical learning, stochastic modeling, and signal processing for vision problems.
